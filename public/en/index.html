A

bout us

Nestled in the historic heart of Paris and two steps from Champs-Elysees and Arc de Triomphe, one of Paris best Avenues. EatMeat is a French Halal Restaurant offering a wide range of Burgers, Sandwichs and Dishes to its customers.

Our Restaurant welcomes you in its warm and friendly setting to make you discover and explore specialties that we continue to improve thanks to you and your returns.
Since its creation in 2005, EatMeat Champs-Elysée has remained faithful to its values while ensuring the best of services. Alone, In a group, or as part of a business meal, our will is to be at your service and offer you the best in terms of quality ...
